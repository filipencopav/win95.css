What is it?

Win95.css is an automatic styler for websites

Ease of deployment

You can include it in your projects in 3 easy steps

  1. Download win95.css
  2. Link it in your <head> via <link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="path/to/win95.css">
  3. Enjoy!
